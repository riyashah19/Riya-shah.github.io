Triboelectric Nanogenerator

⚡️ Measuring voltage with Oscilloscope
Fully functioning TENG prototype
Date January - February 2026

Project Overview

- Designed the structural encasement and base in SolidWorks for the wind-powered Triboelectric Nanogenerator, ensuring precise alignment for the rotating fan shaft and stationary electrode framework for reliable operation

- Researched the triboelectric series and analysed optimal material properties to recommend electrode pairings to the team, targeting generation of the highest static charge and voltage output.

- Applied electromechanical principles to assemble the device and test the circuitry, successfully demonstrating the conversion of mechanical wind energy into a measurable voltage output

- Successfully generated 4V of electricity, meaasured using the oscilloscope, demonstrating the potential of the TENG design for low-power energy harvesting applications
